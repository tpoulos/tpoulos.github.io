Octolingo

Videochat with a universal translator
  • Hackathon project: MHacks 2014
  • Designer and frontend developer on a two person team

Octolingo is a live translator integrated with Google Hangouts. Built at MHacks 2014 in under 24 hours with Tom Shen.

We designed Octolingo to be simple and unobtrusive. The only control is a language picker that sets your preferred language, and the rest is handled automatically. The system can handle up to fifteen people talking in any of the 8 supported languages.
