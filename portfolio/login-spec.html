Expii Login Spec

This is a specification for the expii login system.

Although a spec might seem like the creation of the designer, in reality it’s the product of a long iterative process.

Over time, I’ve come to appreciate the power of a good spec; both to communicate all of the design decisions I’ve made to an engineer and as a tool to help me clarify my own thought process.
