Tele­prompter

Normal teleprompters scroll text at a constant speed, and don’t account adjust their speed if a speaker stops or slows down during their talk. This project is a teleprompter powered by the web speech API, that listens to user's speach and scrolls the text accordingly.

Visit the Github Repo for details on the algorithm I designed keep track of the user’s process, while dealing with the innacuracies of speech recognition.
